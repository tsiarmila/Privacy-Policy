Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 09.09.2024

Thank you for using our app! We take the privacy of our users very seriously, especially when it comes to children under the age of 13. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect the information of children who use our app.

1. Information Collection
Our app may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information: We do not collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. Personal information may include name, email address, or other information that can identify a user.

Automatically Collected Data: We may collect certain data automatically when you use the app, such as device type, IP address, system language, and behavioral data (e.g., actions within the app) through cookies or similar technologies.

2. Use of Information
We use the collected information for the following purposes:

To improve app functionality and user experience.
To provide and support various features of the app.
To respond to user and parent inquiries.
To analyze app performance and identify issues.
We do not use children’s data for advertising purposes and do not share it with third parties for marketing.

3. Parental Consent
For users under 13, we require verified parental consent before collecting personal information. We provide parents with the ability to request access to, correction of, or deletion of their child’s data.

4. Data Access and Disclosure
We do not share user data with third parties except in the following circumstances:

When necessary to perform tasks or services (e.g., technical support).
As required by law (e.g., to comply with legal obligations).
All companies and third-party services we work with are required to adhere to similar privacy standards.

5. Data Security
We take appropriate measures to protect the data we collect, including:

Encrypting data during transmission.
Restricting access to data on a need-to-know basis.
Using secure technologies for server and database protection.
6. User and Parental Rights
Parents have the following rights regarding their child’s data:

Right to Access: You can request information about the data we have collected about your child.
Right to Deletion: You can request the deletion of all data associated with your child.
Right to Opt-Out: You may withdraw consent at any time, which will stop further data collection.
To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If significant changes are made that affect how we handle children’s data, we will notify parents through email or other accessible means. Continued use of the app after such changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
